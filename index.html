Re-Nova Studio

We use Open Labs' Stagelight to capture musical ideas, Soundly for sound effects and foley, iZotope's Advanced Bundle for Audio Edits & Mastering, Waves Plugins and Nuendo 10 for mixing along with Absolute Bundle and Native Instrument's Komplete Collector's Edition for amazing sounds using the awesome Steinberg & Rupert Neve's UR-RT2 Audio interface.

Stagelight Artist

We are part of the Open Labs' Stagelight Artist Community since 2013. We have recorded many tracks on this amazing DAW and use it almost all the time to capture ideas while traveling or anywhere else on using iPad, Android or our laptop workstation. Stagelight is easy to use, has tonnes of amazing sounds from artists like Linkin Park and is available on every platform possible and that is a boon to our workflow.

Explorer - Soundbank

Explorer is our in-progress soundbank. It is an open source project aimed at creating the best collection of great sounding sound effects and clean foley that can be used by artists. Click here to be a part. We are currently designing sounds and recording a lot of foley that will soon be released for all. We are really inspired by the amazing sound banks available in the Soundly Pro subscrption to make a bank of our own.

Original Soundtracks

Re-Nova OSTs is our project on movie background scores based in different genres including orchestration, jazz, fusion and more. Few of the tracks in this project are featured in various short films. We are working on a lot more soundtracks to add to this project that will be released as multiple albums or singles on various platforms. We also do collaboration with artists for this project. Fill the contact us form to join.

Jam & Fun

Our portable studio is filled with new beats and stuff almost all the time. Session contains tracks that were made during experiments. We record almost all of our jam sessions to have tracks that can be added to this project. A lot of those sounds, loops, musical cues also get used to work on the music for our video projects and main releases. We will release this project accessible to all once a good collection is ready.
