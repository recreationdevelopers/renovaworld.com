Re-Nova Music Player


RN Music Player is exclusively available on this page and works on Audio.js designed by Anthony Kobler.
Tracks available here are for free download. Check out Re-Nova on Soundcloud to listen more.

Controls:

Use left () and right () arrow keys to go to previous and next track.
Use spacebar to play/pause the track.
Click on Playlist to see tracks in the queue.

Downloads:

Click on Playlist and select the song you want to download.
Then click on Download to get the selected song for free.
