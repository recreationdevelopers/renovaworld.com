Tracks & Albums

We have compositions in various genres. Every song has been an adventure, a journey towards a better sound. There is a story behind our tracks.

Explorer

Explorer is the idea to create sounds and melodies for various kinds of human emotions. This involves sound synthesis and design to test and apply sound's ability to control our emotions.
A thriller sound can create a sense of curiosity, a warm sound can make people feel at ease. Our aim is to search, create and bring all those sounds into one place and that's why we call this project - EXPLORER.

Re-Nova OSTs

We have been making movie background scores as part of our story telling idea. These Original Soundtracks (OSTs) can be used in short films or scenes as suitable.
Inspired by Hans Zimmer, Ramin Djwadi & Mike Shinoda, we have been adding more tracks to this album since it's first release. As of now we have tracks for thriller, suspense and emotional scenes.

Session

Jamming is fun. Recording while jamming is even better. It helps us to collect all the riffs that we accidently come up with while exploring various instruments and effects.
Session contains tracks that were initially experiments or part of learning new skill. Tracks in this album are and will always be available for free download.

Sounds | Songs | Stories

We have a soundbank, an original soundtrack album and a collection of instrumentals, all available for download. Creating these sounds & songs has been a great experience for us and the supoort from audience has helped us come this far. If these tracks can inspire you and make you feel attached to them, we believe we have done our job. Hope to bring more of these soon.

Release date: Oct 25, 2015
Genre: Electronic
We have been working on a lot of intro music for different videos and love creating them. One of our friend approached us to create an intro for his podcast and we were glad he asked. This track was made keeping in mind the idea that all it takes is a seed to transform into a revolutionary discovery. It is part of our project Re-Nova Jingles. Release date: Aug 3, 2014
Genre: EDM
This song began as part of making an EDM beat. We were playing around with effects like sidechain that lead to the sweeping feel the track has. Then there were piano melodies from our jamming session that we incorporated into the track as bridge. Later on, an orchestral mashup version of this was selected as graVITas 2014 Anthem. Friendship Day was near while we were working on this and it kind of gave a happy feel so we kept the title as 'Friends' and uploaded for download.
